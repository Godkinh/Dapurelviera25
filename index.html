About Us

At WarungMakan, we pride ourselves on offering a variety of delicious and freshly prepared meals that satisfy every craving. Whether you're in the mood for tender grilled chicken, crispy chicken, or flavorful chicken katsu, we've got something for everyone. Our menu also includes seafood fried rice packed with fresh ingredients and the savory, delightful taste of takoyaki straight from Japan's street food scene. Each dish is carefully crafted with the finest ingredients to ensure an unforgettable dining experience. Come and enjoy our welcoming atmosphere and taste the flavors that make WarungMakan stand out!
